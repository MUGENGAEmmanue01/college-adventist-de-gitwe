SEVERAL SPORTS AT GSO BUTARE

We offer a wide variety of sports, catering to the interests and abilities of all students.

Volleyball

Volleyball is an exciting and fast-paced sport at GSO Butare. Students can join our teams for both indoor and beach volleyball.

Football

Football is one of the most popular sports at GSO Butare, with students actively participating in training sessions and inter-school competitions.

Basketball

Our basketball team is well known for its competitive spirit, participating in regional tournaments and showcasing great teamwork.
